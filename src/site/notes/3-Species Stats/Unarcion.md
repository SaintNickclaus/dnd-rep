RP Cost: 11 RP
**Ability Score Modifiers**: Unarcion as a sibling species to Pardarin, gain the same bonuses, +2 bonus to Dexterity, a +2 bonus to Wisdom (Flexible: 2 RP)
**Size**: Medium (0 RP)
**Base Speed**: Unarcion have a base speed of 30 feet (0 RP)
**Languages**: Unarcion start with common Plus Draconic. Unarcion with high [Intelligence](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/basics-ability-scores/ability-scores#TOC-Intelligence-Int-) scores can choose from any of these additional languages: Kyarish, Urisin, Creat or Avian. 

# Defense Racial Traits
---------------------------------------------------------
**Worshipper's Luck** (1 RP): Unarcion gain the following extraordinary ability as a result of their worship and familiarity with the rocky icy cliffs of Glaciersong: Once per day, when a member of this race makes a [Reflex](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/gamemastering/combat#TOC-Reflex) saving throw, it can roll the saving throw twice and take the better result. It must decide to use this ability before attempting the saving throw.
# Feat and Skill Racial Traits
------------------------------------------------------------
**Skill Focus**: Unarcion gain a +2 [racial bonus](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/basics-ability-scores/glossary#TOC-Racial-Bonus) on 2 of the following skills: [Heal](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/skills/heal), [Knowledge (religion)](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/skills/knowledge), [Survival](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/skills/survival), [Knowledge (nature)](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/skills/knowledge), [Climb](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/skills/climb)or [Acrobatics](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/skills/acrobatics)

**Mountaineer (2 RP)**: Unarcion are immune to altitude sickness and do not lose their [Dexterity](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/basics-ability-scores/ability-scores#TOC-Dexterity-Dex-) bonus to AC when making [Climb](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/skills/climb) checks or [Acrobatics](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/skills/acrobatics) checks to cross narrow or slippery surfaces. Unarcion also have a climb speed of 20 feet, and gain the +8 [racial bonus](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/basics-ability-scores/glossary#TOC-Racial-Bonus) on [Climb](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/skills/climb) checks that a climb speed normally grants.

**Silent Hunter (2 RP)**: Members of this race reduce the penalty for using [Stealth](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/skills/stealth) while moving by 5 and can make [Stealth](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/skills/stealth) checks while running at a –20 penalty (this number includes the penalty reduction from this trait).



# Senses Racial Traits
-------------------------------------------------------------
**Darkvision (2RP)**: Unarcion can see in the dark up to 60 feet.

# Alternate Species Traits
------------------------------------------------------------

**Envoy & Emissary** (2 RP): The religious practices of the Unarcion often emphasize aiding others, as a result members can be trained to be able to communicate well with others. (Replaces Silent Hunter)
	- Envoy: Unarcion with an [Intelligence](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/basics-ability-scores/ability-scores#TOC-Intelligence-Int-) score of 11 or higher gain the following [spell-like abilities](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/magic#TOC-Spell-Like-Abilities-Sp-) (the [caster level](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/magic#TOC-Caster-Level) is equal to the user’s character level):
		**1/day**—[comprehend languages](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/magic/all-spells/c/comprehend-languages), [detect magic](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/magic/all-spells/d/detect-magic), [detect poison](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/magic/all-spells/d/detect-poison), [read magic](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/magic/all-spells/r/read-magic).
	- Emissary: Once per day, Unarcion can roll twice when making a [Bluff](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/skills/bluff) or [Diplomacy](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/skills/diplomacy) check and take the better roll.
