**Ability Score Modifiers**: Kanith characters gain a +2 [racial bonus](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/basics-ability-scores/glossary#TOC-Racial-Bonus) to one ability score of their choice at creation to represent their varied nature.
**Size**: Kanith are Medium creatures and thus receive no bonuses or penalties due to their size.
**Base Speed**: Kanith have a base speed of 30 feet.
**Languages**: Kanith begin play speaking Common. Kanith with high [Intelligence](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/basics-ability-scores/ability-scores#TOC-Intelligence-Int-) scores can choose any languages they want (except secret languages, such as Druidic). It also depends on where a Kanith is from 


# Feat and Skill Racial Traits
------------------------------------------------------------
- **Bonus Feat**: Kanith select one extra feat at 1st level.
- **Skilled**: Kanith gain an additional skill rank at first level and one additional rank whenever they gain a level.
