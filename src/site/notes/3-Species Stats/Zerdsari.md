RP Cost: 10
**Ability Score Modifiers**: Zerdsari gain a +2 to Dexterity and Wisdom or a +2 to Dexterity and Strength (Flexible: 2 RP)
**Size**: Small (Canine) as a result they gain a +1 [size bonus](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/basics-ability-scores/glossary#TOC-Size-Bonus) to their AC, a +1 [size bonus](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/basics-ability-scores/glossary#TOC-Size-Bonus) on [attack rolls](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/gamemastering/combat#TOC-Attack-Roll), a â€“1 penalty to their [CMB](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/gamemastering/combat#TOC-Combat-Maneuver-Bonus) and [CMD](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/gamemastering/combat#TOC-Combat-Maneuver-Defense), and a +4 [size bonus](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/basics-ability-scores/glossary#TOC-Size-Bonus) on [Stealth](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/skills/stealth) checks.
**Base Speed**: 30 ft. 
**Languages**: Zerdsari begin play speaking Common, Kyarish & Shen (A sort of sign language). Zerdsari with high [Intelligence](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/basics-ability-scores/ability-scores#TOC-Intelligence-Int-) scores can choose any languages from the following list: Cemic, Avian, Leonin, Urish & Undercommon.


# Defense Racial Traits
---------------------------------------------------------
**Tricksters Luck (1 RP)**: Zerdsari gain the following extraordinary ability: Once per day, when a Zerdsari makes a [Reflex](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/gamemastering/combat#TOC-Reflex) saving throw, it can roll the saving throw twice and take the better result. It must decide to use this ability before attempting the saving throw.

**Fearless (1 RP)**: Zerdsari gain a +2 [racial bonus](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/basics-ability-scores/glossary#TOC-Racial-Bonus) on all saving throws against fear effects.
# Feat and Skill Racial Traits
------------------------------------------------------------
**Skill Focus (2 RP)**: Zerdsari gain a +2 [racial bonus](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/basics-ability-scores/glossary#TOC-Racial-Bonus) on 2 of the following skills from the following list: [Acrobatics](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/skills/acrobatics), [Perception](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/skills/perception), [Perform](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/skills/perform), [Sleight of Hand](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/skills/sleight-of-hand) or [Sleight of Hand](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/skills/sleight-of-hand) 

**Pack Flanking** (4 RP): When an ally adjacent to the Zerdsari hits an enemy with a melee attack, the Zerdsari can take a 5-foot step as an immediate action.
# Alternate Racial Traits
----------------------------------------------------------
**Quick Reactions & Static Bonus Feat (Fleet) (4 RP)**: Zerdsari receive [Improved Initiative](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/feats/combat-feats/improved-initiative-combat) as a bonus feat and 5 extra move speed. 
(Replaces Pack Flanking)
