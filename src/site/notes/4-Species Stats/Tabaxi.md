(Source: [Catfolk](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/RACES/OTHER-RACES/FEATURED-RACES/ARG-Catfolk/#standard_racial_traits))
RP Cost: 6 RP
**Ability Score Modifiers**: Tabaxi are sociable and agile, but often lack common sense. They gain +2 [Dexterity](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/basics-ability-scores/ability-scores#TOC-Dexterity-Dex-), +2 [Charisma](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/basics-ability-scores/ability-scores#TOC-Charisma-Cha-), –2 [Wisdom](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/basics-ability-scores/ability-scores#TOC-Wisdom-Wis-).
**Size**: Tabaxi are Medium creatures and have no bonuses or penalties due to their size.
**Base Speed**: Tabaxi have a base speed of 30 feet.
**Languages**: Tabaxi begin play speaking Common and Tabaxi. Tabaxi with high [Intelligence](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/basics-ability-scores/ability-scores#TOC-Intelligence-Int-) scores can choose from the following languages: Avian, Yern, Cemic, Leonin, and Kotra.

# Defense Racial Traits
---------------------------------------------------------
**Cat’s Luck (Ex)** Once per day when a Tabaxi makes a [Reflex](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/gamemastering/combat#TOC-Reflex) saving throw, he can roll the saving throw twice and take the better result. He must decide to use this ability before the saving throw is attempted.

# Feat and Skill Racial Traits
---
**Natural Hunter**: Tabaxi receive a +2 [racial bonus](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/basics-ability-scores/glossary#TOC-Racial-Bonus) on [Perception](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/skills/perception), [Stealth](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/skills/stealth) and [Survival](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/skills/survival) checks.

# Movement Racial Trait
---
**Sprinter**: Tabaxi gain a 10-foot [racial bonus](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/basics-ability-scores/glossary#TOC-Racial-Bonus) to their speed when using the [charge](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/gamemastering/combat#TOC-Charge), run, or [withdraw](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/gamemastering/combat#TOC-Withdraw) actions.

# Senses Racial Traits
-------------------------------------------------------------
**Low-Light Vision**: Tabaxi have low-light vision allowing them to see twice as far as [humans](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/races/core-races/human) in dim light.
