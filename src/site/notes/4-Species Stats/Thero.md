# Movement Racial Traits
------------------------------------------------------------
**Gliding Wings (3 RP)**: Thero take no damage from falling (as if subject to a constant non-magical [feather fall](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/magic/all-spells/f/feather-fall) spell). While in midair, Thero can move up to 5 feet in any horizontal direction for every 1 foot they fall, at a speed of 60 feet per round. Thero with gliding wings cannot gain height with these wings alone; it merely coasts in other directions as it falls. If subjected to a strong wind or any other effect that causes a creature with gliding wings to rise, it can take advantage of the updraft to increase the distance it can glide.

**Flight (4 RP)**: Thero have a fly speed of 30 feet with clumsy maneuverability.
# Senses Racial Traits
-------------------------------------------------------------
**Low-Light Vision (1 RP)**: Thero can see twice as far as a race with normal vision in conditions of dim light.

# Alternate Racial Traits
---
Thero are of course incredibly varied representing tons of dispositions, professions and talents regardless of where they reside. Some are nocturnal and thus necessitate little rest. Others ignore their natural inclination towards flight and as a result don't develop the capacity to, preferring other academic pursuits. 

**Nocturnal**: Some Thero are immune to magic sleep effects and gain a +2 racial saving throw bonus against enchantment spells and effects.
(Replaces Gilded Wings)

**Learned Arcane Basics** (3 RP): Thero gain a +2 bonus on [caster level](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/magic#TOC-Caster-Level) checks made to overcome [spell resistance](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/gamemastering/special-abilities#TOC-Spell-Resistance). In addition, they also receive a +2 [racial bonus](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/basics-ability-scores/glossary#TOC-Racial-Bonus) on [Spellcraft](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/skills/spellcraft) checks made to identify the properties of magic items.
(Replaces Gilded Wings)

**Gifted Linguist & Urbanite (3 RP)**: Thero gain a +4 [racial bonus](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/basics-ability-scores/glossary#TOC-Racial-Bonus) on [Linguistics](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/skills/linguistics) checks, and they learn one additional language every time they put a rank in the [Linguistics](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/skills/linguistics) skill. They also race gain a +2 [racial bonus](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/basics-ability-scores/glossary#TOC-Racial-Bonus) on [Diplomacy](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/skills/diplomacy) checks made to gather information and [Sense Motive](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/skills/sense-motive) checks made to get a hunch about a social situation.
(Replaces Gilding Wings)

**Theran Arcane Scholar** (4 RP): Thero gain a +2 bonus on [caster level](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/magic#TOC-Caster-Level) checks made to overcome [spell resistance](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/gamemastering/special-abilities#TOC-Spell-Resistance). In addition, they also receive a +2 [racial bonus](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/basics-ability-scores/glossary#TOC-Racial-Bonus) on [Spellcraft](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/skills/spellcraft) checks made to identify the properties of magic items. Thero also gain a +2 [racial bonus](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/basics-ability-scores/glossary#TOC-Racial-Bonus) on [concentration](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/magic#TOC-Concentration) checks made to cast arcane spells defensively.
(Replaces Flight)

**Multitalented** (2 RP): Thero choose two favored classes at 1st level and gain +1 [hit points](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/gamemastering/combat#TOC-Hit-Points) or +1 skill rank whenever they take a level in either of those classes.
(Replaces Flight or Gliding Wings)
