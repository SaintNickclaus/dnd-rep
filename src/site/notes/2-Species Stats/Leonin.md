RP Cost: 10
**Ability Score Modifiers**: Leonin get a +2 bonus on Strength and a +2 bonus to Charisma (Flexible: 2 RP)
**Size**: Humanoid (Big-cat, 0 RP)
**Base Speed**: 30 ft.
**Languages**: Leonin begin play speaking Leonin. Leonin with high [Intelligence](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/basics-ability-scores/ability-scores#TOC-Intelligence-Int-) scores can choose any languages from the following list: Common, Draconic, Avian & Urish

# Feat and Skill Racial Traits
------------------------------------------------------------
**Skill Focus (2 RP)**:  Leonin gain a +2 [racial bonus](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/basics-ability-scores/glossary#TOC-Racial-Bonus) on 2 of the following skills: [Intimidate](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/skills/intimidate), [Knowledge(nobility)](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/skills/knowledge) , [Diplomacy](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/skills/diplomacy) or [Perception](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/skills/perception)

**Terrifying Roar (2 RP)**: - Once per hour, a leonin can, as a standard action, emit a thunderous roar. All creatures within 30ft must make a Will saving throw (DC 10 + 1/2 the leonin's character level + the leonin's charisma modifier) or become shaken for 1d4 rounds. Creatures already shaken become frightened for 1d4 rounds instead. This is a sonic, mind-affecting effect. Creatures that succeed on this save are immune to the same leonin's roar for 24 hours, as are other humanoids with the big cat subtype.

**Claws (2 RP)**: Leonin receive two claw attacks. These are primary natural attacks. The damage is based on the creatureâ€™s size (medium: 1d4).

**Natural Attack (1 RP)**: Leonin receive the ability to preform the [Gore](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/bestiary/rules-for-monsters/universal-monster-rules#TOC-Natural-Attacks) natural attack as a primary natural attack dealing 1d6 damage.

**Fearless (1 RP)**: Leonin gain a +2 [racial bonus](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/basics-ability-scores/glossary#TOC-Racial-Bonus) on all saving throws against fear effects.

# Senses Racial Traits
-------------------------------------------------------------
**Darkvision (2 RP)**: Leonin can see in the dark up to 60 feet.

# Alternate Racial Traits
---
**Ferocity (4 RP)**: Some Leonin have the following extraordinary ability: If the [hit points](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/gamemastering/combat#TOC-Hit-Points) Leonin falls below 0 but is not yet [dead](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/gamemastering/conditions#TOC-Dead), it can continue to fight. If it does, it is [staggered](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/gamemastering/conditions#TOC-Staggered), and loses 1 hit point each round. It still dies when its [hit points](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/gamemastering/combat#TOC-Hit-Points) reach a negative amount equal to its [Constitution](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/basics-ability-scores/ability-scores#TOC-Constitution-Con-) score.
(Replaces Claws and Terrifying Roar)

**Skill Focus (Zenaran Socialite)**: Some Leonin aren't really the fighting type and thus have access to a different set of skills, these Leonin get a +2 racial bonus to two of the following skills: [Knowledge (history)](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/skills/knowledge) , [Acrobatics](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/skills/acrobatics), [Profession (custom)](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/skills/profession), or [Disguise](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/skills/disguise)
(Replaces either Claws, Terrifying Roar or existing Skill Focus)
