RP Cost: 10 RP
**Ability Score Modifiers**: Pardarin gain a +2 bonus to Dexterity, a +2 bonus to Wisdom (Flexible: 2 RP)
**Size**: Medium (0 RP)
**Type**: Humanoid (Big-cat, 0 RP)
**Base Speed**: Pardarin have a base speed of 30 feet (0 RP)
**Languages**: Pardarin start with Common plus Leonin. Pardarin with high [Intelligence](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/basics-ability-scores/ability-scores#TOC-Intelligence-Int-) scores can choose from any of these additional languages: Avian, Runuan, Kyarish and Urish


# Feat and Skill Racial Traits
------------------------------------------------------------
**Swift as Shadows** (3 RP): Pardarin reduce the penalty for using [Stealth](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/skills/stealth) while moving at full speed by 5, and reduce the [Stealth](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/skills/stealth) check penalty for sniping by 10.

**Skill Focus**: Pardarin are often well tuned hunters which often rely on having a good lay of the land. This of course doesn't mean those skills can't be applied in other contexts, Pardarin gain a +2 [racial bonus](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/basics-ability-scores/glossary#TOC-Racial-Bonus) on 2 of the following skills: [Knowledge (geography)](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/skills/knowledge), [Perception](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/skills/perception), [Survival](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/skills/survival), [Stealth](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/skills/stealth) [Knowledge (nature)](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/skills/knowledge), or [Acrobatics](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/skills/acrobatics)

**Bite (2 RP)**: Pardarin gain a natural bite attack, dealing damage equivalent 1d6
# Senses Racial Traits
-------------------------------------------------------------
**Darkvision 60 Feet** (2 RP): Members of this race can see in the dark up to 60 feet.
