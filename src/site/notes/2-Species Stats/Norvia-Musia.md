(Source: [Ratfolk](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/races/other-races/featured-races/arg-ratfolk/))
RP Cost: 9 RP
**Ability Score Modifiers**: Norvia-Musia are agile and clever, yet physically weak. They gain +2 [Dexterity](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/basics-ability-scores/ability-scores#TOC-Dexterity-Dex-), +2 [Intelligence](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/basics-ability-scores/ability-scores#TOC-Intelligence-Int-), and –2 [Strength](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/classes/core-classes/cleric/domains/paizo-domains/strength-domain).
**Size**: Norvia-Musia are Small and thus gain a +1 [size bonus](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/basics-ability-scores/glossary#TOC-Size-Bonus) to their AC, a +1 [size bonus](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/basics-ability-scores/glossary#TOC-Size-Bonus) on [attack rolls](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/gamemastering/combat#TOC-Attack-Roll), a –1 penalty on [combat maneuver](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/gamemastering/combat#TOC-Combat-Maneuvers) checks and to their [CMD](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/gamemastering/combat#TOC-Combat-Maneuver-Defense), and a +4 [size bonus](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/basics-ability-scores/glossary#TOC-Size-Bonus) on [Stealth](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/skills/stealth) checks.
**Base Speed**: Norvia-Musia have a base speed of 20 ft.
**Languages**: Norvia-Musia begin play speaking Common. Norvia-Musia with high [Intelligence](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/basics-ability-scores/ability-scores#TOC-Intelligence-Int-) scores can choose from the following: Cemic, Draconic, Stornish, Kyarish, Cemic, Urish, Avian, and Undercommon.

# Offense Racial Traits
---------------------------------------------------------
**Pack Flanking**: When an ally adjacent to the ratfolk hits an enemy with a melee attack, the ratfolk can take a 5-foot step as an immediate action. (4 RP)

# Feat and Skill Racial Traits
------------------------------------------------------------
**Skill Focus**: Norvia-Musia gain a +2 [racial bonus](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/basics-ability-scores/glossary#TOC-Racial-Bonus) on three of the following skills: ([Craft](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/skills/craft) (custom), [Perception](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/skills/perception), [Use Magic Device](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/skills/use-magic-device),  [Disable Device](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/skills/disable-device) or 
# Senses Racial Traits
-------------------------------------------------------------
**Darkvision**: Norvia-Musia can see perfectly in the dark up to 60 feet.

# Alternate Feat(s)
----
**Skill Focus** (Rodent Empathy): Norvia-Musia gain a +4 [racial bonus](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/basics-ability-scores/glossary#TOC-Racial-Bonus) on [Diplomacy](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/skills/diplomacy) and [Bluff](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/skills/bluff) checks made to influence other rodent species. (Replaces pre-existing skill focus)

**Skill Focus** (Rodent Rascal): Some Norvia-Musia use their small and slender physique to hone their finesse at tasks which require sensitivity. They gain a +3 [racial bonus](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/basics-ability-scores/glossary#TOC-Racial-Bonus) in one of the following skills: ([Craft](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/skills/craft) (Custom), [Sleight of Hand](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/skills/sleight-of-hand), [Heal](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/skills/heal) and [Escape Artist](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/skills/escape-artist))
(This replaces pre-existing Skill Focus)

**Bite**: Some Norvia-Musia are born with particularly large incisors, capable of injuring others, these rodents gain access to a Bite attack. The bite is a primary attack, or a secondary attack if the creature is wielding manufactured weapons, dealing 1d4 damage. 
(Replace Swarming)
