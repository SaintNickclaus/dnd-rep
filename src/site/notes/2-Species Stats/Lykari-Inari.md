RP Cost: 9
**Ability Score Modifiers**: Lykari-Inari gain a +2 to Dexterity and +2 to Charima or a +2 to Dexterity and a +2 to Wisdom. (Flexible: 2 RP)
**Size**: Medium (Canine)
**Base Speed**: 30 ft
**Languages**: Lykari-Inari begin play speaking Common & Kyarish. Lykari with high [Intelligence](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/basics-ability-scores/ability-scores#TOC-Intelligence-Int-) scores can choose any languages from the following list: Cemic, Avian, Leonin, Runuan, Urish, Stornish, Draconic & Undercommon.


# Feat and Skill Racial Traits
------------------------------------------------------------
**Skill Focus** (2 RP): Lykari-Inari gain a +2 [racial bonus](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/basics-ability-scores/glossary#TOC-Racial-Bonus) to 2 of the following  [Appraise](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/skills/appraise), [Diplomacy](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/skills/diplomacy), [Bluff](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/skills/bluff), or [Profession (Custom)](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/skills/profession) 

**Skill Focus (Tricks of the Trade)** (3 RP): Lykari-Inari spend much of their time honing their skills due to that, they can a +3 [racial bonus](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/basics-ability-scores/glossary#TOC-Racial-Bonus) to one of the following skills: [Appraise](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/skills/appraise), [Diplomacy](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/skills/diplomacy), [Bluff](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/skills/bluff),  [Profession (Custom)](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/skills/profession) , [Perform](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/skills/perform), [Sense Motive](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/skills/sense-motive),  [Disguise](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/skills/disguise) or [Knowledge (Custom)](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/skills/knowledge)

**Emissary (1 RP)**: Once per day, Lykari-Inari can roll twice when making a [Bluff](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/skills/bluff) or [Diplomacy](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/skills/diplomacy) check and take the better roll.


# Senses Racial Traits
-------------------------------------------------------------
**Darkvision 60 Feet (2 RP)**: Lykari-Inari can see in the dark up to 60 feet.

# Alternate Racial Traits
---
**Curiosity (4 RP)**: Lykari-Inari are naturally inquisitive about the world around them. They gain a +4 bonus on [Diplomacy](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/skills/diplomacy) checks to gather information, and [Knowledge](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/skills/knowledge) (history) and [Knowledge](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/skills/knowledge) (local) become class skills for them. If they choose a class that has either of these [Knowledge](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/skills/knowledge) skills as class skills, they gain a +2 [racial bonus](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/basics-ability-scores/glossary#TOC-Racial-Bonus) on those skills instead.
(Replaces Skill Focus (Tricks of the Trade) and Emissary)

**Flexible Bonus Feat (4 RP)**: Lykari-Inari select one extra feat at 1st level.
(Replaces Skill Focus (Tricks of the Trade) and Emissary)

**Envoy (1 RP)**:  Lykari-Inari with an [Intelligence](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/basics-ability-scores/ability-scores#TOC-Intelligence-Int-) score of 11 or higher gain the following [spell-like abilities](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/magic#TOC-Spell-Like-Abilities-Sp-) (the [caster level](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/magic#TOC-Caster-Level) is equal to the user’s character level):
	**1/day**—[comprehend languages](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/magic/all-spells/c/comprehend-languages), [detect magic](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/magic/all-spells/d/detect-magic), [detect poison](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/magic/all-spells/d/detect-poison), [read magic](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/magic/all-spells/r/read-magic).
(Replaces Emissary)

**Sociable (1 RP)**: When Lykari-Inari attempt to change a creature’s attitude with a [Diplomacy](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/skills/diplomacy) check and fail by 5 or more, they can try to influence the creature a second time even if 24 hours have not passed.
(Replaces Emissary)
